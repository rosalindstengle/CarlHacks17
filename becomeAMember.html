Want to join us?

The basic level annual membership for the Northfield Garden Club is $15 a year. Because we are affiliated with the MN State Horticultural Society (MHSH), a joint membership entitles you to a reduced rate subscription to the Northern Gardener, the bi-monthly magazine published by the MSHS . For a cost of $52 you can purchase a joint membership ($37 for MSHS and $15 for our local club). Contact the Garden Club President or membership representative for information about membership or just mail your check to the address above. Please be sure to include your contact information including your mailing address, phone number and email.
