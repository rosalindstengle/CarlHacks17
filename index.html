Welcome!

Welcome to the Northfield Garden Club website.

Whether you are interested in learning more about gardening, wishing to be involved in helping to beautify the Northfield area, or just wanting to interact with other garden enthusiasts, the Northfield Garden Club will meet your needs. Please contact any of the members of the board of directors for information on our activities. Since 1950, The Northfield Garden Club has endeavored to advance and promote the art and science of horticulture and encourage all forms of home and civic g ardening projects in the Northfield, Minnesota, area.
